# Day-by-Day Dinner Instructions

## MONDAY – Baked Fish with Vegetables
**From Fridge:** 6 cod fillets, herb butter, cut vegetables, yogurt sauce, lemon-garlic sauce, cooked rice  
**From Pantry:** Walnuts

**Quick Steps (10 min active):**
1. Preheat oven to 400°F. Place seasoned fish and vegetables on sheet pan (5 min)
2. Bake 20 min. Meanwhile, reheat rice in microwave (2 min active)
3. Serve with sauces on side, garnish with walnuts

**Pack Lunches:** 2 adult portions in containers

---

## TUESDAY – Slow-Cooker Chicken Tikka Masala
**From Fridge:** Slow-cooker insert, cilantro-yogurt  
**From Pantry:** Naan bread  
**From Freezer:** Green peas (500g/1 lb)

**Quick Steps (8 min active):**
1. Transfer insert to slow-cooker base, turn to LOW for 6 hours (2 min)
2. Add peas in last 30 min (1 min)
3. Warm naan, serve with cilantro-yogurt garnish (5 min)

**Pack Lunches:** 3 adult portions in containers

---

## WEDNESDAY – Sheet-Pan Honey-Mustard Chicken
**From Fridge:** Marinated chicken, prepared sheet pan, honey-mustard glaze  
**From Pantry:** Grated parmesan

**Quick Steps (15 min active):**
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (3 min)
2. Bake sheet pan 20 min (2 min active)
3. Brush glaze on adults' portions, add parmesan, bake 5 more min (5 min)

**Pack Lunches:** 2 adult portions; save extra sweet potatoes for Thursday

---

## THURSDAY – Black Bean & Sweet Potato Bowls
**From Fridge:** Cooked black beans, leftover sweet potatoes, rice, chipotle crema  
**Fresh:** 3 avocados  
**From Pantry:** Tortillas, grated cheddar, cumin, lime

**Quick Assembly (10 min):**
1. Heat beans with cumin and lime juice (4 min)
2. Warm rice and sweet potatoes in microwave (3 min)
3. Assemble bowls with cheese, avocado, chipotle crema for adults

**Pack Lunches:** None – no leftovers

---

## FRIDAY – Turkey Bolognese with Hidden Veggies
**From Fridge:** Bolognese sauce base, grated veggies, chopped spinach  
**Fresh:** Ground turkey (1.8 kg/4 lbs)  
**From Pantry:** Pasta (500g/1.1 lbs), grated parmesan

**Quick Steps (20 min active):**
1. Brown all turkey with grated veggies, add sauce base (10 min)
2. Boil water, cook pasta while sauce simmers (12 min total, 5 min active)
3. Add spinach to sauce last minute, serve with parmesan

**Pack Lunches:** 2 adult portions; reserve 4 portions for Sunday dinner

---

## SATURDAY – Eat-Out Night
Restaurant meal – no prep needed!

---

## SUNDAY – Pasta Bake with Garlic Bread
**From Fridge:** Friday's leftover bolognese (4 portions)  
**From Freezer:** Prepared garlic bread  
**From Pantry:** Grated mozzarella

**Quick Assembly (10 min):**
1. Mix pasta and sauce in baking dish, top with mozzarella (2 min)
2. Bake at 375°F for 8 min
3. Toast garlic bread 2 min, serve

**Pack Lunches:** None – no leftovers
