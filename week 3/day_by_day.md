# DAILY DINNER INSTRUCTIONS

## MONDAY - Baked Salmon & Vegetables (20 min)
**From Prep:** Pre-seasoned salmon, cut vegetables, cooked rice  
**Additional:** Yogurt, lemon wedges

1. Preheat oven to 400°F/200°C. Place salmon and vegetables on sheet pan.
2. Bake 15-18 minutes until salmon flakes easily.
3. Serve over reheated rice (microwave 2 min) with yogurt side.

**Pack Lunches:** 2 adult portions in containers for Tuesday

---

## TUESDAY - Slow Cooker Chicken Curry (15 min morning/evening)
**From Prep:** Cubed chicken, curry sauce, cauliflower, rice  
**Additional:** Canned tomatoes, spinach (buy fresh), yogurt

Morning:
1. Add chicken, curry sauce, cauliflower, and tomatoes to slow cooker.
2. Cook on LOW for 6 hours.

Evening:
1. Stir in fresh spinach, cook 10 minutes.
2. Serve over reheated rice with yogurt.

**Pack Lunches:** 2 adult portions for Wednesday

---

## WEDNESDAY - Lentil Pasta Bolognese (10 min)
**From Prep:** Cooked lentils, diced vegetables, tomatoes, parmesan  
**Additional:** Pasta, canned tomato sauce

1. Boil pasta according to package (8-10 min).
2. Heat lentils with vegetables and tomato sauce (5 min while pasta cooks).
3. Toss pasta with sauce, top with parmesan.

**No lunch leftovers planned**

---

## THURSDAY - Turkey Taco Bowls (10 min)
**From Prep:** Seasoned turkey, rice (from Tuesday), cheese, tomatoes  
**Additional:** Lettuce, salsa, sour cream, tortillas

1. Reheat turkey and rice in microwave (3 min).
2. Chop lettuce, arrange bowl ingredients.
3. Assemble bowls with all toppings, warm tortillas if desired.

**No lunch leftovers planned**

---

## FRIDAY - Sheet Pan Chicken & Vegetables (20 min)
**From Prep:** Seasoned chicken strips, cut vegetables  
**Additional:** Whole grain rolls, milk

1. Preheat oven to 425°F/220°C. Arrange chicken and vegetables on sheet pan.
2. Bake 20 minutes until chicken reaches 165°F/74°C.
3. Warm rolls last 3 minutes, serve with milk.

**Pack Lunches:** 4 adult portions for weekend

---

## SATURDAY - Eat Out Night
Family restaurant outing - no prep needed!

---

## SUNDAY - White Bean Minestrone (35 min)
**From Prep:** Cooked beans, diced vegetables  
**Additional:** Pasta, spinach, canned tomatoes, broth, parmesan, walnuts

1. Sauté carrots and green beans in oil (5 min).
2. Add beans, broth, tomatoes, simmer 20 min.
3. Add pasta and spinach last 10 min, top with parmesan and walnuts.

**Pack Lunches:** 3 adult portions for Monday
