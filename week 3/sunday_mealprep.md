# SUNDAY MEAL PREP GUIDE (2.5 hours)

## PREP STATION SETUP
- Large cutting boards (2)
- Sharp knives
- Storage containers (labeled)
- Pressure cooker
- Rice cooker

## BATCH COOKING STEPS

### STEP 1: Pressure Cooker Beans & Lentils (45 min)
1. **White beans:** 400g/14oz dry + 4 cups water, HIGH 30 min → Store: "SUN-BEANS"
2. **Red lentils:** 400g/14oz dry + 3 cups water, HIGH 12 min → Store: "WED-LENTILS"
3. Natural release 10 min each

### STEP 2: Rice Cooker (30 min concurrent)
- 2.5 cups brown rice + 5 cups water
- Divide when done:
  - 2 cups → "RICE-MON" container
  - 3 cups → "RICE-TUE" container  
  - 3 cups → "RICE-THU" container

### STEP 3: Proteins (20 min)
| Protein | Preparation | Storage | Can Mix With |
|---------|------------|---------|--------------|
| Salmon (600g) | Season with dill, garlic, lemon zest | Airtight, fridge, "MON-SALMON" | Keep separate |
| Chicken thighs (900g) | Cube, salt lightly | Airtight, fridge, "TUE-CHICKEN" | Keep separate |
| Chicken breasts (900g) | Cut strips, season paprika | Airtight, fridge, "FRI-CHICKEN" | Keep separate |
| Ground turkey (600g) | Brown with taco seasoning | Airtight, fridge, "THU-TURKEY" | Already mixed |

### STEP 4: Vegetables (30 min)
| Vegetable | Amount | Cut Style | Storage | Can Combine |
|----------|--------|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Bell peppers MON | 2 | Strips | "MON-VEG" | With broccoli |
| Broccoli | 300g | Florets | "MON-VEG" | With peppers |
| Cauliflower | 400g | Florets | "TUE-CAULI" | Keep separate |
| Carrots WED | 2 | Small dice | "WED-VEG" | With celery |
| Celery | 2 stalks | Small dice | "WED-VEG" | With carrots |
| Bell peppers FRI | 3 | Strips | "FRI-VEG" | With green beans |
| Green beans FRI | 400g | Trimmed | "FRI-VEG" | With peppers |
| Carrots FRI | 2 | Rounds | "FRI-VEG" | With above |
| Tomatoes WED | 400g | Halved | "TOMATOES-WED" | Keep separate |
| Tomatoes THU | 200g | Halved | "TOMATOES-THU" | Keep separate |
| Carrots SUN | 1 | Dice | "SUN-VEG" | With green beans |
| Green beans SUN | 200g | 1-inch pieces | "SUN-VEG" | With carrots |

### STEP 5: Sauces & Seasonings (15 min)
**Curry Sauce:** Blend garlic (4 cloves), ginger (2"), curry powder (2 tbsp), cumin (1 tsp), coconut milk → Glass jar "CURRY-SAUCE"

**Taco Mix:** Combine chili powder (3 tbsp), cumin (1.5 tbsp), paprika (1 tbsp), oregano (2 tsp) → Jar "TACO-MIX"

### STEP 6: Cheese & Omega-3 (15 min)
- Grate cheddar (300g) → "CHEESE-CHEDDAR"
- Grate mozzarella (200g) → "CHEESE-MOZZ"  
- Grate parmesan (150g) → "CHEESE-PARM"
- Grind flaxseed (4 tbsp) → "FLAX"
- Portion chia seeds → "CHIA"
- Chop walnuts → "WALNUTS"

## STORAGE GUIDELINES
**Fridge (use within 5 days):** All proteins, cooked grains, cut vegetables, cheese  
**Counter:** Whole grain rolls, tortillas  
**Buy mid-week:** Spinach (Thursday), lettuce (Wednesday)

## COMBINED STORAGE NOTES
✓ **CAN combine in same container:** Vegetables going in same dish  
✗ **Keep SEPARATE:** Raw proteins, different meal components  
✓ **Stack similar:** All cheese containers, all rice containers
